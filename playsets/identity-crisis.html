Identity Crisis - What Role Were You Again?

This playset has the potential for a lot of unknown role shenanigans.

Roles

  • President/Bomber
  • Dcotor/Engineer
  • r/b Spy
  • r/b Coy Boy
  • Hot Potato
  • Leprechaun
  • Drunk
  • MI6 (if even number of players)
  • Blue and Red Team (as needed to fill player count)

Explanation

To start, it includes the base roles of President and Bomber, and their Doctor and Engineer. Because the main roles will have to full share with the respective secondary roles, this encourages card sharing.

Included are Spies and Coy Boys on each team. Though card sharing is encouraged, the spies keep everyone on their toes and the coy boys give them a reasonable way to "hide."

Also added are Hot Potato and Leprechaun. While both roles mean an instant win or loss for whoever holds them, they are instantly traded when color- or card-shared. The Drunk adds in more to this combination, resulting in a player who doesn't know who they are until the final round.

Depending on player counts, the rest are filled with basic Blue Team and Red Team roles. If there is an even number of players, the MI6 can be added to balance teams out.
