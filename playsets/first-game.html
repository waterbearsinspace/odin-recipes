The First Game - An Introduction to Two Rooms and a Boom

This playset serves as a solid introductory game for new players.

Roles

  • President/Bomber
  • Doctor/Engineer
  • r/b Spy
  • r/b Coy Boy
  • Romeo/Juliet
  • MI6 (if odd number of players)
  • Blue and Red Team (as needed to fill player count)

Explanation

To start, it includes the base roles of President and Bomber, and their Doctor and Engineer. Because the main roles will have to full share with the respective secondary roles, this encourages card sharing.

Included are Spies and Coy Boys on each team. Though card sharing is encouraged, the spies keep everyone on their toes and the coy boys give them a reasonable way to "hide."

Also added are Romeo and Juliet. These are great grey roles that have a fun idea behind them that's easy to understand.

Depending on player counts, the rest are filled with basic Blue Team and Red Team roles. If there is an odd number of players, the MI6 can be added to balance teams out.
